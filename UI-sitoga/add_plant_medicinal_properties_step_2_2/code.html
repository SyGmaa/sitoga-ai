Aloe Vera

Medicinal Plant

Benefits

Pain Relief Digestive Aid Immune Support

Preparation

Dry the leaves in a cool, shaded area for 48 hours until crisp.

Grind the dried leaves into a fine powder using a mortar and pestle.

Mix one teaspoon of powder with hot water and let steep for 5 minutes.

warning Precaution

High doses may cause stomach discomfort. Pregnant women should consult a doctor before use.
